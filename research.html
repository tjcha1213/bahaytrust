Research Buckets 📊

The key areas to document and compare.

Demand patterns

Population density, migration into major cities, student and early-career renter demand, household budgets, and commute tradeoffs.

Supply dynamics

Unit availability, landlord fragmentation, informal inventory, and the economics of small-format rentals.

Platform landscape

Existing listing platforms, niche operators, classifieds, and offline channels currently shaping the search experience.

User trust issues

Fraud risk, low listing quality, incomplete information, inconsistent verification, and poor fit between listing and lived reality.
