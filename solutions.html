Solutions

Designed for renters, landlords, brokers, and redevelopment decision-makers.

Roomie is not just a listing interface. It is a set of connected tools and advisory capabilities built around the realities of dense urban housing markets.

Solution 01

Microhousing platform

A web and mobile-accessible platform for microhousing that reaches renters, landlords, brokers, and other market users through cleaner discovery, stronger trust signals, and verified information.

The current interface direction is visible through the product demo used for user testing and workflow review.

View UI Demo

Solution 02

Market dashboard software

Dashboard software that derives practical and deeply useful metrics for landlords and brokers using world-class real estate and housing-policy research, geospatial analysis, and spatial statistics.

The goal is not abstract analytics, but clearer understanding of demand, neighborhood conditions, access, and market shifts that can improve real operating decisions.

Solution 03

Redevelopment strategy and consultation

Redevelopment strategies and consultation based on real-time data and research grounded in in-depth field work by real estate optimization analysts.

This work is aimed at helping stakeholders evaluate how underperforming or misaligned housing assets can be repositioned with stronger market fit and better long-term value.

Why It Matters

Each solution strengthens the others.

Trust

The platform makes verified information and housing context more visible to users.

Insight

The dashboard turns research, geospatial analysis, and spatial statistics into operational judgment.

Action

The advisory layer uses real-time data and field work to support redevelopment and optimization decisions.
