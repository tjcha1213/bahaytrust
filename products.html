Core Product

A platform shaped around the realities of dense urban renting.

Roomie is not just trying to list smaller units. The product is meant to make microhousing easier to discover, compare, trust, and operate in markets where mobility, density, and affordability all matter at once.

Renter discovery

A cleaner search and discovery experience for compact units, including stronger context around location, surrounding access, and the practical tradeoffs of dense urban living.

Trust signals

Better ways to evaluate listing quality, unit credibility, and the reliability of information presented to renters.

Operator workflows

More structured tools for owners and operators managing smaller units across fast-moving city neighborhoods.
